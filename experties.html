Our MEP Systems Expertise

At P K Technology, we specialize in the execution of a wide range of Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) systems, offering end-to-end solutions that meet the highest industry standards. Our services are designed to provide efficiency, safety, and long-term reliability across various sectors and building types.
We have extensive experience in delivering the following systems:
Fire Fighting Systems – Complete solutions including Hydrant Networks, Automatic Sprinkler Systems, and Pumping Stations designed to ensure rapid and effective fire suppression.
*Fire Fighting Equipment & Fire Extinguishers – Supply, installation, and maintenance of a comprehensive range of fire extinguishers and related firefighting equipment compliant with safety regulations.
*Fire Detection and Alarm Systems (Smoke/Heat) – Advanced detection systems featuring smoke and heat sensors to provide early warnings and safeguard lives and assets.
* Public Addressing & Talk Back Systems – High-quality communication systems for emergency announcements, paging, and two-way communication within facilities.
* High Tension (HT) and Low Tension (LT) Power Systems – Design and installation of power distribution networks, substations, and low-side electrical works for safe and reliable energy supply.
* Plumbing Systems – Complete solutions for water supply, drainage networks, roof drainage, wastewater management, and sanitary fittings, ensuring efficient and hygienic water management.

Specialized GEBERIT System Execution

In addition to our core MEP capabilities, we have specialized expertise in the execution of advanced GEBERIT systems, which are globally recognized for their innovation and efficiency. Our services include:
* HDPE Syphonic (Pulvia) Roof Water Drainage System – High-performance roof drainage solutions designed for maximum efficiency in handling large volumes of rainwater with minimal downpipes.
* HDPE Single Stack SOVENT Waste Drainage System – Space-saving and efficient drainage technology that reduces the number of stacks required while maintaining optimal flow rates.
* Geberit Toilets, Sanitary Fixtures & Accessories – Supply and installation of premium-quality sanitary products, designed for both durability and modern aesthetics.
* HDPE Pipe Systems – Our team is fully trained and highly experienced in the professional execution of HDPE pipe networks, ensuring precise installation, leak-proof joints, and long service life.
